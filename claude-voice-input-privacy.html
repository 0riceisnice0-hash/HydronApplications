Privacy Policy for Claude Voice Input

Last updated: 29 March 2026

What we collect

We collect your email address and password when you create an account. We store a login token in your browser to keep you signed in. We do not store your audio recordings. Audio is sent to OpenAI's Whisper API for transcription and immediately discarded after the text is returned.

How we use your data

Your email is used solely to identify your account and manage your subscription. We do not sell, share or transfer your data to any third party except OpenAI for the purpose of transcription, and Stripe for payment processing.

Third parties

We use OpenAI Whisper for audio transcription. We use Stripe for subscription payments. Both operate under their own privacy policies.

Data retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time by contacting us.
